Here are some common Frisbee violations and what they mean:

Violation Reason
Foul The player got in a collision with someone else that caused them to drop the disc
Strip The disc got knocked out of the thrower's hand when they didn't mean to throw it
Travel Thrower took unnessesary steps while holding the disc
Walk-In/Out Player walked into the endzone/out of bounds while holding the disc
Stall Player took more than 10 seconds to throw the disc
